About Us

WHAT WE DO?

We provide visual stories, product videos, advertisements, animation videos, social media elements and 3D modelling on the virtual world.

We also provide services apart from the virtual realm. Setting up home theatres, studio rooms, virtual classrooms and other audio video services.

OUR APPROACH

Our fully capable team turns your thoughts into material. Think of it as giving us a few grains of rice to get your hot meal!

We help you turn your concepts into a carefully curated script which then gives us a storyboard to work upon. We take full responsibility for all pre-production details such as casting, charting, location hunt etc. It doesn't end there, we also get the shoot and the post production done to a T, giving you the perfect product you envisioned.
